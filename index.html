Data Analytics Dashboard Predicting Credit Defaulting

I created a dashboard for Home Credit using data obtained from the Kaggle competition. The main objective was to predict the factors that contribute to applicants defaulting on a loan. Through the dashboard, I discovered that applicants who had previously been denied a loan were more likely to default on a loan, despite having higher income. Undergraduate Thesis Work Using Python, Tableau, and Excel

I undertook a project over the course of three semesters to analyze the animation industry and its impact on economic development. My research aimed to prove that the animation industry could be used as a tool for economic growth, and to do so, I had to create the dataset myself due to the novel nature of the research. I used data from The World Bank database, The Park Database, and Google Trends to conduct my research. My extensive analysis led me to a significant conclusion that the animation industry has a profound impact on economic development. Moreover, it could serve as a valuable tool for policymakers to target development.
